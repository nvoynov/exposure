The world has shrunk to the boundaries of a neighborhood. A storm of propaganda tears the "Self" apart, a wall of checkpoints on the borders. Yet this neighborhood is still a fractal piece of the entire universe.

That is how this photo project was born. A long journey inward. Forest, lake, bicycle. The changing of seasons, sun and clouds, wind, fog, and snow. A person looks, enters the scene, becomes a part of it. The scene gazes back, observes, and lets them in. A quiet song to the place — my companion on this journey.

Slow down. Free your attention. Take it in. Enter the scene... Rest, look closely at your own "around you." Express it, share it with the world.
