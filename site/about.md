## Exposure

Exposure is an open space for my photographic series. The main idea behind it is to build a quiet environment meant to strip away the noise of modern digital feeds, letting each series breathe.

Photography is not my professional career. It is one of my childhood hobbies, which suddenly returned in my middle years and gradually grew into something deeper. This website is the result of reaching a turning point where I felt it was the right time to share.
